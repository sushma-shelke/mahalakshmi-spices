About Us

Welcome to Original Mahalaxmi Masale

We Organic khandeshi food products through our reputed brand "original mahalaxmi", we bring tradition, taste, and quality together to create authentic spice blends loved by families across generations. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision—to offer pure, high-quality masalas that enrich every meal with flavor and authenticity. Mahalaxmi Masale was established with the belief that the essence of great cooking lies in the quality of its ingredients. Our founders, guided by passion and dedication, laid the foundation for a company that would become synonymous with trust and quality. The vision was simple yet profound: to provide families with spice blends that capture the true flavors of Indian cooking. Our journey began in humble surroundings, driven by a desire to share the joy of authentic flavors with every household. Over the years, through dedication and hard work, Mahalaxmi Masale has grown into a respected brand that stands for quality, purity, and taste.

Punam Balasaheb Hembade

From 2010 to today

Smt. Poonam Hembade learned the art of making original Mahalaxmi Mirchi spices, along with the nuances of teamwork and supporting local, needy, and illiterate women, from her mother, Mrs. Babybai Hembade, during her childhood. In 2010, she officially took over the Mahalaxmi Mirchi Spices business and began her entrepreneurial journey.

Embracing the digital age, Smt. Poonam Hembade has modernized operations, handling everything from order management to feedback and salary payments online. She has upheld a commitment to social welfare, focusing on providing employment opportunities to women, even selling goods at a no-profit, no-loss basis to support local women. Special preference is given to widows, abandoned women, and women from lower castes, promoting inclusivity and empowerment.

The original Mahalaxmi Mirchi spices, crafted using the traditional Khandeshi method, are renowned for their quality. This is achieved through the home method of spice preparation, maximizing the use of handmade materials, conserving electricity, and using fresh, coarse spices, ensuring a superior product at competitive prices.

Under her leadership, the business has adapted to modern requirements by registering on the GeM (Government e-Marketplace) portal, developing technology for e-tenders, and facilitating global deliveries through the website. Additionally, she has introduced an Android application to ensure transparency in worker attendance, salaries, and other operational aspects, along with an application for tracking sold goods and sales activities. This blend of tradition and modernization has solidified the brand's commitment to quality and social responsibility.
